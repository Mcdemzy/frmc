About Us

Fortune Royal Multi-Concept, founded in 2022 is a training platform with the sole purpose of equipping individuals with the much-needed skills to thrive in business. We've trained over 5,000 individuals globally and worked with different firms and organizations in Nigeria. We've received many awards from firms like the Association of Business Educators of Nigeria, the Nigerian Journal of Business Education (NIGJBED), etc. We are certified under the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC).
